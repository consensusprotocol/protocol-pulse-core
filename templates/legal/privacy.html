Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Protocol Pulse respects your privacy and operates on a minimization principle: collect only what is needed to deliver the Signal Terminal, intelligence briefs, and account services.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email, password hash, and profile preferences when you register.
  • Operational logs: request metadata (IP, user agent, route, timestamp) for security and uptime diagnostics.
  • Session data: cookie/session tokens used to keep authenticated sessions active.

How We Use Data

We use data to authenticate users, secure the platform, process premium access, improve reliability, and deliver relevant product functionality. We do not sell personal data.

Third-Party Processors

We may rely on payment, infrastructure, and notification providers to run services. Those providers process data under their own policies and contractual obligations.

Security

We apply standard safeguards including hashed credentials, encrypted transport (HTTPS), and access controls. No online system is risk-free, but hardening and monitoring are continuously maintained.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of account-related data by contacting support. We process requests within a reasonable operational window.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy as services evolve. Material changes are reflected by an updated effective date on this page.
